About:

Climbing and Trailing plants grown indoors will need to be clipped back on occasion to restrain their rigorous lengths. This will also encourage a bushier mien and foster more blooms. Pruning is best done in the spring before the onset of new growth. If the plants are a really rapid grower, you may need to prune again in the fall. Prune just above a node or swelling where a leaf was.

Sunlight:

Tend to thrive in low light

Water:

Water when soil is complely dry

Temperature:

As low as -30 ° F
