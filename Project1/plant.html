SUCCULENTS

Succulent Foliage Plant Fern Climber and Trailer Plant Flowering Plant

About:

Succulents are any plant with thick fleshy tissues adapted to water storage. Some succulents store water only in the stem and have no leaves or very small leaves, whereas others store water mainly in the leaves.

Sunlight:

At least 6 hours of indirect sunlight

Water:

Water when soil is complely dry

Temperature:

Between 60 ° F and 80 ° F
